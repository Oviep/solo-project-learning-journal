ABOUT ME

Hi there! My name is Ovie and welcome to my learning journal.

After several months of learning in the Front-End Developer Career Path, I've made the big jump to the Bootcamp to get expert code reviews of my Solo projects and meet like minded peers

How I stay commited to learning

I like to think of myself as a lifelong learner. I used two spend hours and hours learning, then try and create simple projects using what I learned or work on new techniques into existing projects.
While that was fun, I felt like it would be helpful to share what I was learning and most things about my journey with the world.

How I got started

I started simple and gradually grew my learning journal site. I would take notes about what I was learning. After each learning session, I'd use my notes to not only reflect on what I learned but also write short summaries of what I learned using my own words.
That helped me grow what I was learning and I realised that posting my learning summaries was also helping others learn and stay motivated.
